About YMMOR 2025

Bringing Together Early Career Researchers in Model Order Reduction

Conference Aims

The Young Mathematicians in Model Order Reduction conference aims at bringing together early career researchers working in the broad field of Model Order Reduction and related topics.

Conference Format

Every participant is expected to give a talk about their research or a topic of interest. Small introductory sessions will facilitate communication across different specializations.

Who Should Attend?

The conference is designed for PhD students, master students, and early postdocs with basic knowledge in Model Order Reduction and related fields.
